# [!DNL Meta Ads] campaign settings

You can't create [!DNL Meta Ads] campaigns in Search, Social, & Commerce. You can edit minimal campaign settings.

## [!UICONTROL Campaign Details]

**[!UICONTROL Campaign Name]:** A campaign name that's unique within the account. The maximum length is 15 double-byte characters.

<!-- **[!UICONTROL Start Date]:** -->

{{$include /help/_includes/start-date.md}}

<!-- **[!UICONTROL End Date]:** -->

**[!UICONTROL Status]:** The display status of the campaign: *Active* or *Paused*.

## [!UICONTROL Campaign Tracking]

<!-- **[!UICONTROL Override Account Tracking]:** -->

{{$include /help/_includes/override-account-tracking.md}}

**[!UICONTROL Tracking Config]: [!UICONTROL Enable conversion reporting in Adobe Analytics]:** (Advertisers [with [!DNL Adobe Analytics for Advertising]](/help/integrations/analytics/overview.md) and with an Adobe Analytics report suite specified in the account settings) Appends the [AMO ID (s_kwcid) tracking parameter](/help/search-social-commerce/tracking/amo-id-tracking-parameter.md) to all ads in the campaign. The parameter enables Adobe Analytics to associate conversions with Meta Ads campaigns in the report suite that's specified in the account settings.
