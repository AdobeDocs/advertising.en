# (New UI) Rename a portfolio

*Beta feature*

Quickly rename a portfolio without opening the full portfolio settings.

1. In the main menu, click **[!UICONTROL Manage] > [!UICONTROL Portfolios]**.

1. Hold the cursor over the portfolio row and click **[!UICONTROL ...] > [!UICONTROL Rename]**.

1. Edit the name, and then click **[!UICONTROL Apply]**.
