# Change the Status of a Google Conversion Value Rule

[!BADGE Beta Feature]{type=Informative}

You can pause, activate, or delete a conversion value rule in accounts and campaigns for which conversions are tracked at the individual account level.

1. In the main menu, click Optimization > Conversion Value Rules.

   By default, the submenu is set to Account to show all account-level rules.

1. (Optional) To view all campaign-level rules instead, select Campaign in the submenu.
1. Select the check box next to each row to change.
1. In the toolbar, do any of the following:
   * To activate (enable) paused rules, click ![Activate](/help/search-social-commerce/assets/activate.png "Activate").
   * To pause active rules, click ![Pause](/help/search-social-commerce/assets/pause.png "Pause").
   * To delete active or paused rules, click ![Delete](/help/search-social-commerce/assets/delete.png "Delete").
1. In the confirmation message, click Activate, Pause, or Delete, depending on the status.
