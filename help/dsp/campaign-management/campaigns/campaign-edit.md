# Edit a campaign

1. In the main menu, click **[!UICONTROL Campaigns]**.
1. Next to the campaign name, click  **[!UICONTROL ...]** > **[!UICONTROL Edit]**.
1. Edit the [campaign settings](campaign-settings.md).
1. Click **[!UICONTROL Save Campaign]**.
