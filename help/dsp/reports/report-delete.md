# Delete a Custom Report

1. In the main menu, click **[!UICONTROL Reports]** > **[!UICONTROL Custom Reports]**.

1. Next to the report name, click **[!UICONTROL ...]** > **[!UICONTROL Delete]**.

1. In the confirmation message, click **[!UICONTROL Delete]**.
